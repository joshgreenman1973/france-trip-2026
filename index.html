Yarn Shopping in France

France has a wonderful knitting culture. Parisian yarn shops tend to carry high-quality French brands (Fonty, De Rerum Natura, La Bien Aimee, Laines Plassard) plus European favorites. Many shops are small and don't have websites, so check hours before going.

Paris Yarn Shops

Les Tricoteurs Volants

One of the best indie yarn shops in Paris. Natural fibers — wool, mohair, silk, alpaca, linen, hemp — with emphasis on French brands and indie hand-dyed yarns. Owner Enrico is passionate and welcoming. Workshops ~€35/2hrs. Staff friendly even if you don't speak French.

22 Rue de la Fidelite, 75010 (near Gare de l'Est)

French Yarn Brands to Look For

  • De Rerum Natura — French wool from French sheep. Gorgeous natural and dyed colors. Sustainable and traceable
  • La Bien Aimee — Indie hand-dyed. Sophisticated palette. Has a Paris shop (listed above)
  • Fonty — Historic French mill (since 1880). Beautiful classic yarns at fair prices
  • Laines Plassard — French-made, wide range. Available in many merceries
  • Bergere de France — Major French brand. Affordable, good quality basics

French law requires car seats for children under 10/under 135cm — including in taxis. However, there is a legal exemption: if a taxi has no car seat, children over 3 may ride without one on short urban trips, belted in the back.

For the Orly airport run (highway driving): pre-book a taxi that confirms a car seat. Don’t rely on the exemption for fast-road trips. For short city hops (Gare de Lyon to hotel, etc.), the exemption applies in practice.

  • Lajoieway — From ~€50 (Orly). Free car seat included. lajoieway.com
  • Family Transport Paris — Mercedes Classe V, fits up to 3 car seats. familytransportparis.com
  • Taxi Bambino — Specializes in families. taxibambino.com
  • G7 Famille — Paris’s biggest taxi company. “G7 Family” option. g7.fr
