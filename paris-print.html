Montmartre

Hilly, charming, village-feel neighborhood. Sacré-Cœur (free, dome €7), Place du Tertre artists' square (caricatures ~€20–30), funicular (€2.15), Wall of Love. Metro: Line 5 dir. Bobigny → Gare du Nord, Line 2 dir. Porte Dauphine → Anvers.

Afternoon ideas

Parc des Buttes-Chaumont / Canal Saint-Martin / Paris Zoo

Buttes-Chaumont: wild hilly park with a temple on a rocky island. Great for running around, less touristy.
Canal Saint-Martin: walk along the locks, watch boats, trendy cafés.
Paris Zoo (Parc Zoologique): modern zoo in Bois de Vincennes with 5 biozones. Family pass €68 (2 adults + 2 kids). Plan 2+ hours. Metro Line 8 dir. Créteil → Porte Dorée (6 stops).

Toy Store · Montmartre area

Car Seat Taxi Services

  • G7 Famille — biggest Paris taxi co. G7 app or +33 1 47 39 80 00. Select "G7 Family"
  • Taxi Bambino — family specialist. taxibambino.com. Book 24h ahead
  • Family Transport Paris — Mercedes vans, up to 3 car seats. familytransportparis.com
  • Marcel — app-based, select "Family" option. marcel.cab
  • Taxi Baby Seat — minivans, 1–8 passengers. taxibabyseat.com

Metro Tips

  • Navigo Easy: reloadable card, t+ tickets ~€2.15 each
  • Under 4: free · Ages 4–9: half price · 10+: full price
  • Carnet of 10: buy 10 tickets at once for a discount
  • Many stations lack elevators. RER and bus are more accessible with a stroller
  • Watch for pickpockets on Line 1, at Châtelet, and on RER B

Money & Tipping

  • Euro (EUR). ~1 USD = 0.90–0.95 EUR
  • Visa/Mastercard widely accepted. Have cash for markets
  • Use bank ATMs (BNP, Crédit Agricole). Avoid Euronet
  • Tipping: service included. Rounding up is appreciated, not expected
  • Tax-free shopping on purchases over €100 — ask for détaxe form

Eating with Kids

  • Lunch 12–2 PM, dinner 7:30–9 PM. Kitchens close between meals
  • Menus enfants at most restaurants (~€8–12)
  • Boulangeries: your best friend. Pain au chocolat = dream breakfast
  • "Une carafe d'eau" = free tap water
  • Bread comes with every meal, free
